Electronic Resources Services

1. HYPERMEDIA CENTRE SERVICES

National Library of Malaysia facilitates library users with Hypermedia Centre Service. The Hypermedia Centre is placed at Level 4, Anjung Bestari Building. There are several services and facilities in Hypermedia Centre:


Computer, Internet and WIFI




Hypermedia Centre facilitates library users with multimedia computers and free internet services. All library users would access internet easily, search and download information, enjoy space to complete their works, tasks, assignments and organize training session. Free Wifi services available within all location spot at the Hypermedia Centre let the library users enjoy internet with their own laptop and tablet.


Screening Room




The Hypermedia Centre accommodates one Screening Room to facilitate library users for watching TV programmes, films, stories, cartoons and jokes. However library users have to make reservation for the vacant room.


Multimedia Players and Devices




There are a few TV, cassette players, CD and DVD players in the hypermedia centre.


Audio Visual and Electronic Resources Reference




Library users can make reference and research onto the collection of audio visual and electronic resources in various subjects. There is collection in the field of managements, computer sciences, religions, social sciences, languages, technologies, arts, histories and other subjects. The publishers for the materials are locally and internationally. These materials can be made a reference in the form of CD, DVD, and cassette for audio and video and other format.


Digital Library Services




The National Library offers library users a Digital Library at u-Pustaka portal (url:http://www.u-library.gov.my) Library users would enjoy read and made reference for eBooks, eMagazines, eJournals and ePapers at u-Pustaka the Digital Library. Library users can access u-Pustaka portal to the information from databases subscribed by National Library of. The users can access National Library’s digital contents through u-Pustaka portal and must be a member of the portal to allow them access to the full contents of digital resources.


Video on Demand



Library users can watch films or movies for their entertainment. These entertainments would be call as Video on Demand Service. There offered several types of films which are fictions, non-fictions, cartoons, for jokes or the latest movies that published locally or internationally.

2. CYBER ZONE SERVICES

National Library of Malaysia facilitates library users with Cyber Zone Service. The Cyber Zone is placed at Level 2, Anjung Bestari Building. There are several services and facilities that offered freely in Cyber Zone:


Computers and Internet



This Cyber Zone facilitates library users with forty (40) multimedia computers and high speed internet. Library users can made use these computers for their own works, assignments, for their small business, entrepreneur and virtual secretary.


Tablets/ iPad



There are nine (9) tablets or i-Pad available for library users to access internet, information search and reference. Library users are also encouraged to access the mobile apps in order to access all online databases subscribed by library.

3. PNM SMART LIBRARY AND INFORMATION CORNER FOR PEOPLE WITH DISABILITIES

National Library of Malaysia has facilitates users with PNM Smart Library and Information Corner for People with Disabilities (OKU). The aim of this service is to support the national agenda to transform Malaysia into an information and communication technology or the national agenda to transform Malaysia into an information and communication technology or ICT proficient and information rich society. It is designed to full fill the community growing demands for read and reference interactively.


Zones in PNM Samsung Smart Library:


CHILDREN CORNER



This corner facilitates all children at age 12 years old and below with Samsung’s tablets and AR’s books, the Augmented Reality service.


INTERACTIVE ZONE



The zone facilitates library users with four (4) tablets and two (2) Large Format Display, LFD Screens to access ePapers and eMagazines. The titles for ePapers are The Star and Malay Mail. The titles for eMagazines are Life is Beautiful, Jelita, Dashboard, Dinar & Darham, Cube, Keluarga Aman, Afro Rider, Yours Discovery and National Geographic.


READING ZONE



The zone facilitates library users with thirty-two (32) tablets. All these tablets let library users read eBooks conveniently. There are various topics and subjects available for eBooks’ readers.


WORKING ZONE



The zone facilitates library users with seven (7) laptops and four (4) multimedia computers. All these computer types’ devices are equipped with Microsoft Office, E-Studio and Cloud Reader. Library users can made use these facilities for their own works, assignments, for their small business, entrepreneur and virtual secretary.
