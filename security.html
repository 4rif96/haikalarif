PNM Security Policy

× The contribution and dissemination of prohibited information and negative materials that contradict the policies of the State are prohibited.
× Disseminating politically charged information, incitement, or racial content, or anything that undermines the reputation of the National Library of Malaysia and the Public Service is prohibited.
× All information intended to be uploaded to the website of the National Library of Malaysia must obtain approval from the Head of Department/Division.
× Any acts of intrusion and attempts to hack into the website of the National Library of Malaysia are prohibited.
